What is Civis Mundi?

Civis Mundi is a GitHub project that was created to explore technical solutions to the problem of the portability of foreign credit history. You can read more about this on our Projects page. There you can learn some of the reasons why this is a difficult solution space to enter.

In the process of our work, we've become somewhat expert on the topic of Open Banking integrations. This is something we continue to explore.
